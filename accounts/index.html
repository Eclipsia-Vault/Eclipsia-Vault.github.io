Manage Your Nuvio Account

nuvio account sync

Sign in to your Nuvio account to add Eclipsia and manage synced account.

This is not an official Nuvio page. Your email and password are sent directly to Nuvio's own backend over HTTPS — this page never stores your password. Everything below talks directly to your Nuvio account's public API using your own session token.

What happens when you sign in here
  • Your credentials go straight to Nuvio's Servers over HTTPS — they never pass through or get logged by this site itself.
  • Your session token is kept in this browser tab only (sessionStorage, not localStorage), so it's cleared automatically when you close the tab.
  • Every action below is scoped to your own account and only touches the resource you explicitly edit (a single profile's plugins, a single profile's library, etc.).
  • Full-replace actions are labeled clearly. Addons, plugins, library, profiles, and collections use "full replace" sync — removing something from a list and saving deletes it from your account. Watch progress and watch history use non-destructive merge, so pushes never delete existing entries; use the explicit Delete button for those.
  • No analytics or tracking scripts run on this page — the site's Content-Security-Policy only allows requests to Nuvio's backend and nowhere else.
  • Prefer not to enter your password on a third-party page? You can always add the plugin manually from the homepage install guide instead — no account needed.

For full account settings — password/email changes, full profile settings, and more — visit nuvio.tv/account/login directly on Nuvio's own site.
